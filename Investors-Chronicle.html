Investors Chronicle

Art Direction, Editorial Illustration

Investors’ Chronicle is a weekly magazine aimed at private investors, produced and published by the Financial Times Group.

From April 2021 to April 2023, I served as one of the lead designers for the IC cover. I collaborated closely with the Editor, to translate briefs into compelling cover illustrations delivered on a biweekly basis.

In addition to cover design, I was responsible for the branding of events, podcasts, and awards. I also partnered with the lead artworker to enhance the marketing asset templates for both digital and print formats.

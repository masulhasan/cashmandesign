SustainableViews

Branding

SustainableViews is a service for financial services professionals from the Financial Times. It aims to be the authoritative source of news on developments in sustainability policy and regulation.

I collaborated closely with the editor of SustainableViews to create a look and feel for the service that highlights this core aim.

SustainableViews has evolved from a specialized email part of Investors’ Chronicle to a standalone product.
